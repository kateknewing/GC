Pace of Game

How quickly the game progresses determines the difficulty of gathering data. A high mark are awarded for high-speed games where data collection by hand would be difficult and a low mark is awarded where the game is slow-paced and recording data is not difficult.

Benefit of Automation

This measures if a sport can benefit from automating certain aspects of recording game data. A high mark is awarded if the sport has many aspects of recording data that can be automated by GameChanger and a low mark is awarded if automation is not beneficial for facilitating scoring.

Low Medium High
No aspect of game play or scorekeeping can be automated by use of e-scoring. Automation could only be utilized for a limited number of game aspects Automation of game situations could greatly ease burden or scorekeeping.
