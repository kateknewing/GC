Initial Scoping


To narrow down the sports market of over 90 sports we began looking at high school data from the NFHS (National Federation of State High School Association). This data provided insight into the relative size of the amateur sports market from each state between the years 2002 – 2013. While GameChanger’s current clientele is much wider than high school, we believe the high school sports market to be a fair indication of the trends in youth sports. The first on the list of priorities for GameChanger when evaluating criteria for expansion is market size. Of the over 90 sports, the overwhelming majority have such low participation rates that an expansion would be useless.

In order to better understand which sports would be viable options, we began by took the sport-by-sport participation divided by the total participation. We concluding that without having 2% of the high school market share, it was an irrelevant option for GameChanger’s expansion.
