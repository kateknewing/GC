Benefit of Live Updates

Sports with a structure that support periodic updates would lead to more subscriptions to GameChanger's premium version, which offers live game updates. Sports that are essentially captured in the final score would not gain much benefit from live updates.

Competition

There are sports apps available that will compete with GC for market share and the user base. A high mark is awarded for sports with few or ineffective scoring apps and a low mark is awarded for sports with a many or high-quality scoring apps.
