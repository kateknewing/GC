Culture

Subfactor Breakdown

Appreciation for Statistics

For a sport to be viable for a data collection app, the coaches, parents and general culture of the sport must place a value on statistics. A high mark is awarded to sports that regularly collect and utilize stats and a low mark was awarded for sports that generally ignore stats or don't think they are useful.

Low Medium High
Data provides little to no opportunities for advanced analytics. No value placed on statistics in evaluation and strategy. Opportunities exist for advanced analytics but are not yet widely accepted or valued. High value placed on analytics. Advanced statistics are used widely for game strategy.
Acceptance of Technology

This factor is essential for determining promising sports to expand into. A high mark is awarded for the members and culture of a sport being open to implementing new technology and a low mark is given for sports that prefer to avoid technology and play the game with minimal changes.

Low Medium High
Resistant to use of technology within the sport. Moving towards integration of technology in the sport. Early adopters are using some. Widely accepted integration of technology in the game.
Demand for Content

Sports that would benefit from play-by-play updates or statistical tidbits would make good candidates for GameChanger. A high mark is awarded for sports that would benefit from play-by-play commentary and are stat-heavy and a low mark was awarded for slow-paced sports without many stats to record or display.
