Volleyball

Pros
  • Many tedious aspects of official scorekeeping can be automated
  • Emergence of culture of innovation and advanced statistics as evidenced by professional leagues
  • Structure of sport lends itself to possibility for demand of play by play reporting
Cons
  • Rudimentary existing scoring apps exist
  • Advanced statistics use in strategy is only just maturing

Factor Analysis

Market Size and Demographics

Volleyball has seen the highest participation growth rate at the high school level within the last 5 years compared to other sports. According to the SFIA 2015 report on volleyball participation, there are 6,304,000 volleyball participants in the United States.

The gender split in the sport is about 60% female participants and 40% male participants. About 60% of players are below the age of 24, with 13-17 year olds being the dominant age range of the population.

Geographically, volleyball is most popular along the East Coast. Specifically the regions of the South Atlantic, Mid Atlantic and East North Central.

Organizational Structure

Governing Body Requirements: High

The main governing body of amateur volleyball is USA Volleyball (USAV). At games there is an official scorekeeper and sometimes a secondary scorekeeper. The official scorekeeper is responsible for collecting and recording the starting lineup from each team at the start of each game.

Some tournaments use software called Advanced Entertainment Systems (AES) to manage their tournament matching and event schedules. AES has an app on the app store but many users complain about the presence of ads.

Team versus Individual Scoring: High

Each game is played head to head between two teams, however at the more advanced level of league games they are usually part of larger tournaments where many teams come together and play on multiple courts.

The score of the game is kept for the team overall. The official scorekeeper is only responsible for keeping track of the overall score of the game, not individual points or statistics.

Team Size: Medium

Volleyball teams are moderately sized compared to other sports, the minimum team size is eight players but there can be up to 16.

Team Composition: High

For official scoring, each position is tracked same way on the scoresheet except for the position of the libero. The libero is a special position in indoor volleyball; the libero must be in the game at all times and is not limited by the rules of rotation. The scorekeeper must make a special mark on the scoresheet whenever the libero serves.

Culture

Acceptance of Technology: Medium

There are rudimentary score keeping apps that exist for Volleyball which only let users keep track of the overall score. According to our interviews, it is not uncommon for parents to keep their own score during games using these apps to ensure the official score keeping is accurate.

At the professional level, companies such as VolleyMetrics record videos of games and give statistical analysis to accompany the footage. However, this service is very expensive and not accessible to amateur leagues.

Demand for Content: Medium

The gameplay style in volleyball would benefit from a play by play update system and based on our interviews there would be an interest in that type of reporting. However, implementing a system could prove difficult because of the pace of the game. There are existing products for post-game metrics in the professional market but not in the amateur markets.

Appreciation for Statistics: High

Collection of individual player statistics are present within the professional level reporting. For amateur leagues, the assistant manager will often keep track of individual statistics. However, there is no standardized set of statistics that are recorded or advanced statistics that are calculated. The benefit of the statistics in strategy also varies, as some teams do not utilize statistics to assist their gameplay or training.

Pain Points

Game Clock: Low

No game clock is kept within volleyball

Field Position: High

The order and position of players within volleyball is very important. This is something that the official scorekeeper keeps track of and it is vital to the serving order of the game.

Pace of Game: High

Volleyball is an extremely high paced game. This can lead to many mistakes being made by the official scorekeeper if they are not experienced. There is a lot of offered training and videos teaching people how to score in volleyball, therefore there could be benefit in the practice scorekeeping tutorials that GameChanger offers.

Benefit from Automation: High

The scorekeeping method of volleyball is very tedious and many of the aspects of scorekeeping could be made more efficient through digitalization. The scorekeeper marks off each point that is made by the team by crossing of numbers that are printed on the sheet itself.

He or she must also make a special mark every time the libero has to serve. When substitutions are made there must be a mark to indicate which player was subbed and what the score was when the substitution happens. In general, there are multiple marks that have to be made when certain things occur during the game that could be automated by an app.
